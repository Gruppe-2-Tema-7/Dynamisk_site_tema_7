About us

Meet Luca Santorini

The heart behind Chop Chop is Luca Santorini, a passionate home cook from a small coastal town in southern Italy. Growing up, he spent hours in his grandmother’s kitchen, learning the secrets of traditional Mediterranean cooking. For Luca, food isn’t just about eating—it’s about sharing, connecting, and keeping traditions alive.

Behind Chop Chop

After traveling through Greece and Spain, Luca was inspired to create Chop Chop as a way to bring authentic Mediterranean flavors to home kitchens everywhere. His goal? To make Mediterranean cooking simple, fun, and accessible for everyone.

Now, it’s your turn! Pick a recipe, start chopping, and enjoy the magic of Mediterranean cuisine.
